Agriculture disease prediction and management system in crops using drones with AI

Mohan Babu University

Innovating today for a brighter tomorrow

Description

Description: In agriculture sector we are using drones for spraying the pesticides to the entire field without any idea about disease prone areas, It results in decreasing yeild of the crop.So drones can predict the disease prone areas before it occurs by using the various micro organisms present in the crop, with the help of lens. Based on some algorithms we can scan the pictures and predict the disease with a clear solution and it sprays the pesticides in affected area.
